// Who I Am

About

I'm Simon John. Formally, I study English Linguistics to understand the underlying architecture of how we communicate. Practically, I write code to push the boundaries of what we can do with that understanding.

For me, syntax trees and software architecture aren't that different. Whether I'm analysing a massive text corpus for research or structuring an offline-first database for a Flutter app, my goal is always to turn complex, unstructured data into clean, accessible experiences. Outside of the terminal, you'll likely find me diving into game soundtracks, learning Japanese, or looking for the next problem to solve.

I'm currently based in Germany, open to collaboration, research partnerships and interesting conversations.
